We have over 20 years of combined experience on agriculture and ICT.


Shamba Records revolutionizes agriculture in Sub-Saharan Africa by bridging the digital divide through innovative agtech solutions. Our comprehensive suite of software and hardware tools includes a cutting-edge real-time data collection system. This system not only gathers crucial production data from farmers but also seamlessly connects them to aggregation centers and markets.

One of our flagship features is the automation of farmer payments, credit scoring, credit issuance, market linkage, and agriculture remote extension services. This streamlines the entire agricultural value chain, making it more efficient and profitable for everyone involved.

At the heart of Shamba Records is our commitment to transparency and accountability. We leverage blockchain technology to eradicate ghost farmers and prevent manipulation of farm and harvest records. Additionally, our smart contracts ensure secure and transparent transactions between farmers, especially those involved in leasing farms to each other. This revolutionary approach not only enhances trust within the agricultural community but also fosters a more sustainable and equitable farming ecosystem.
